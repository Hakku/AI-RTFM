## FAQ

### Q: How do I get access to [system]?

Submit an access request at [link]. Your manager must approve. Typical turnaround: [X hours/days].

### Q: What do I do if I break something?

Don't panic. Notify the team in #team-channel immediately. Follow the [incident response process](../standards/review-process.md). Every mistake is a learning opportunity.

### Q: When will I be added to the on-call rotation?

After completing on-call shadowing (usually week 3) and manager sign-off. You'll start as secondary on-call.
